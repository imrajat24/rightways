Expert Guidance Session (EGS)

Core feature

EGS is our Mentorship program in which an experienced professional of a particular domain (Our Experts) guides a less experienced person (Our Users) and help them to resolve their queries in that domain.

Our Experts will guide you on the following points -

  • Brief introduction of the domain.
  • Real time experience in the domain.
  • How to pursue a career in the domain.
  • How to sustain and excel in the domain.
  • Future opportunities in the domain.
